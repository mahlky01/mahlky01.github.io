Kyle Mahlkuch

Kyle Mahlkuch is a student at Luther College. Kyle is a Math Education and Computer Science major. Kyle participates in band at Luther. He plays saxophone in the Wind and Percussion ensemble. During the summers Kyle works at Sugar Creek Bible Camp. He councels Middles School age children for one week at a time at the camp which is located in Ferryville WI, near La Crosse. Photos and information about Sugar Creek can be seen in the "Kyle's time at Sugar Creek" link.
