Sugar Creek

Kyle Worked at Sugar Creek for the last two summers. He works with primarily Middle school aged kids.
He and his campers do various activities like camping in cabins, cooking over fires, playing games and learning about the bible.
Sugar Creek is located in Ferryville WI near the Mississippi river. Campers go on many canoe trips and on house boats up and down the river.
